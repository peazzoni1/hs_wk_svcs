Frequently Asked Questions
  • I'm interested to find out if my student is a candidate for the program, How do I get started?
    Please contact us to schedule a meeting.

  • What age can students begin Transitional Employment?
    Students as young as 16 may be eligble.

  • Is transportation provided to worksites?
    Yes. Transportation is arranged through the school.

  • Does employment continue through the Summer?
    In some cases a student may be able to work year round.

  • How long does it take for a student to join a worksite?
    The process typically takes about 2 months from the initial assessment.
